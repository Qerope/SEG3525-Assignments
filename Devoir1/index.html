How I Work

01

User-Centered Process

I start by deeply understanding user needs through research and stakeholder interviews.

02

Iterative Design

I create wireframes and prototypes (low‐ to high‐fidelity), gather feedback through usability tests, and refine designs in rapid cycles.

03

Collaboration & Communication

I work closely with product managers, developers, and end users—maintaining clear documentation, design systems, and style guides for consistency.

04

Agile & Data-Driven

I integrate analytics and A/B testing to validate design decisions, ensuring ongoing optimization of usability, performance, and accessibility.

05

Front-End Prototyping

I build interactive prototypes using React (or Figma's interactive components) to demonstrate functionality and transitions before full development.
